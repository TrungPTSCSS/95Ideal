Introduce
We at 95IDEAL TECH are an advanced research facility with modern facilities, equipment and workspace. We are committed to providing the best working environment to ensure the success of research projects.
Facilities and Equipment
We are equipped with the most advanced and modern equipment to support research activities. We invest in the latest technologies and ensure that our devices are always up to date and performing at their best.
Working space
We provide comfortable and modern working spaces for researchers. Our workspace is designed to create the best conditions for creativity and personal growth. We believe that a comfortable and trustworthy working environment is an important factor in achieving research success.
Virtual Tour
We provide virtual tour experiences through videos and animations. This way, you can explore our lab and see the equipment and workspaces visually. We hope that the virtual tour experience will help you better understand our lab and build confidence in participating in research projects.
If you are interested in visiting our lab, please contact us for more detailed information and to schedule a tour.
