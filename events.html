Ben Tre INNOVATION DAY
Ben Tre province's innovation festival in 2022 "BenTre Innovation Day-2022" takes place with a series of consecutive events such as: Hackathon competition for solutions and creative ideas to solve local or social problems; "Innovation in yourself" business innovation competition; Ben Tre CEO Forum "Solutions to create smart Ben Tre"; Pitching Businesses and Investors. Along with STEM creative experience activities; display and introduce models of innovation and digital transformation; "Factory Innovation Tour" innovation tour.
